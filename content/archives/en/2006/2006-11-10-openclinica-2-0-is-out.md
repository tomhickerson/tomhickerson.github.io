---
title: "OpenClinica 2.0 is out"
date: 2006-11-10
---

<P>If you are interested in clinical data management and Java EE, check out <a href="http://www.openclinica.org/">OpenClinica</a>, which has just released its 2.0 version. A webinar is scheduled for <a href="http://www.openclinica.org/entities/entity\_details.php?eid=295">November 17th</a>, so if you are looking for an open-source, Java-based solution for handling clinical research in a hospital, lab, or other facility that handles PHI, check it out.
