---
title: "Steve Jobs: advice to entrepreneurs"
date: 2007-01-18
---

<P><a href="http://kottke.org/">Kottke</a> points to a <a href="http://americanhistory.si.edu/collections/comphist/sj1.html">1995 interview with Steve Jobs</a> over at the Smithsonian. His advice to future and budding entrepreneurs is timeless: <P><code>I'm convinced that about half of what separates the successful entrepreneurs from the non-successful ones is pure perseverance. It is so hard. You put so much of your life into this thing....So you've got to have an idea, or a problem or a wrong that you want to right that you're passionate about otherwise you're not going to have the perseverance to stick it through. I think that's half the battle right there.</code>
