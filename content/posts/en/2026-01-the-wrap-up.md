Much along the same lines as last year, I'm leaving here the full list of what I watched and read in 2025.  As far as entertainment went, 2025 was pretty good.  I was pretty busy with work and current events, but what I did see was mostly stellar.

### Highlights

+ Severance Seasons One and Two.  I didn't get the hype when the first season came out but then I caught up this year, and it was thoroughly engrossing. 
+ Noir Fiction.  I started picking up books by Raymond Chandler and Dashiell Hammett and found them to be cracking reads, once you get around all the subtle racism, sexism, homophobia, and smoking.
+ Stranger Things Season Five.  Such a quality end to a quality series.  As someone who graduated in the class of 1989, the whole thing was a lovely tribute to the 80s.
+ Common Side Effects.  As someone who works in the clinical sciences, I found this an absolutely hilarious dark comedy.  I actually watched the season ender first and then went back and watched the whole season.  

Other things I have on my list to watch from 2025:

+ Andor Season Two.  (I know, I KNOW)
+ The rest of Star Trek Strange New Worlds.  Starfleet Academy is just kicking off and I find that I'll probably need to finally check out some of the last episodes of Discovery Season Five to make sure I'm properly caught up.  And maybe Prodigy Season Two, since the Holo-Doctor is in there, but that's a lot of Trek to take in. 
+ Pluribus.

### Lowlights

+ And Just Like That.  The thing about editing is that, when it's great it's invisible but when it ain't, you definitely see it.  Especially in the last episode when the news had already broken that the series was cancelled. 
+ Star Wars: Tales of the Underworld.  For something that came out on May the Fourth, I would have expected... well, just better.  Visions came out later that year and made up for it (mostly).
