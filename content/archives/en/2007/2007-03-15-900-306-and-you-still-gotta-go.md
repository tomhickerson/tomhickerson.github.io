---
title: "900-306, and you still gotta go"
date: 2007-03-15
---

<P>Just read off of <a href="http://www.austinist.com/archives/2007/03/14/news\_bits.php">Austinist</a>: Lady Longhorns Coach Jody Conradt <a href="http://sportsillustrated.cnn.com/2007/basketball/ncaa/wires/03/12/2060.ap.bkw.texas.conradt.5th.ld.writethru.0844/">resigns</a>, after her 900th win as a ladies' basketball coach and 31 seasons at the University of Texas. <P>The reason? No National Championship for the 'Horns since the legendary 34-0 season in 1986. Never mind that her winning to losing record is 900-306 overall. One of the things that brought push to shove was a five-game losing streak in February which was the <i>longest in the UT ladies' basketball program's history</i>. <P>Talk about a bummer. Statistics, apparently, can be damned; you can win 900 times, but lose 5 times in a row and you gotta go.
