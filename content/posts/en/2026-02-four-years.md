I'm writing this post late, because frankly February 24th this time around was much darker than the years before.

Today is actually March 16th.  The weather is nicer, the electricity is (more or less) on all the time, we are beating back Russian forces in the southeast.  However, it's darker in other parts of the world still.

So to lighten the mood, I am posting pics of Kharkiv from much happier times.
